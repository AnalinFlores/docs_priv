How to manage your Folders and files

  • Create,upload new Folder/Text

    Just click on the "+" icon it top of the files list and drop down menu will appear. For exmple, click on the New Folder, then name it and press Enter to confirm. Is the same for the other options that appear in the drop down menu.

  • Review the Folder's/file's details

    Just click on the "..." icon it top of the files list and drop down menu will appear. You can review the file's/folder's details, rename them, move them download and unshare.
