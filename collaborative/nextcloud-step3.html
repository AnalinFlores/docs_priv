Nextcloud

Share folder/files option.

If you click on the share icon of a folder of a file, you'll see something like this:

  1. You can share the file via email or typing the username. The person will receive an email with a download link.
  2. Also you can create a "share link". Check the box and a link will be creaed for your file. You can add some other features to your link, like "Allow editing" and more.

Some recomendations to share folders and files:

  • If you want to share a folder or a file with an external person of the organization, send them an email invitation to download the file.
  • If you wnat to share a folder or a file with a member of the organization, create a download link for him/her
